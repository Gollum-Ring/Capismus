Capism
Capism is a religion founded by Lord Jakob Carl Liss on September 17, 2024. Its followers are called Capists.
Central to Capism is the holy and almighty god Capis, who, according to the Nomos, the holy scripture of Capism, watches over humanity and seeks to guide them back to the "right and holy path."

Holy scripture
The Nomos is the holy scripture of Capism, divided into six Tuves and one Sakris, which in turn are divided into verses.
